Our Story

Located in Perambra, Chalakudy, Swamy's Vaibhavam is a sanctuary for pure vegetarian cuisine. We blend traditional Kerala recipes with modern culinary techniques to create an unforgettable dining experience.

From our signature Cheese Paneer Manchurian Dosa to classic South Indian thalis, every dish is crafted with passion, utilizing the freshest local ingredients and aromatic spices.
